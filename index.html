About us

Who are we?

We are perfectionists. We love what we are doing. We put our heart and soul in the work we do. We aim to leave the world a better place than how we found it yesterday. Importantly we risk failure.

What do we do?

We help retailers play the business game with more control over their inventory and scale their business without compromising on uncertainty. We want to transform the way the grocery industry has been doing business and create the next wave of digital transformation in the grocery industry.

Why do we do this?

The success of the e-commerce giants lies in their ability to make informed decisions. We thought why not give that superpower to the ordinary retailer community? With more power in their hands, they can tend to their customers needs more carefully and can invest in their inventories with more confidence. We respect the contribution of traditional grocers towards the economy and we want to aid them to maximize their contribution.
